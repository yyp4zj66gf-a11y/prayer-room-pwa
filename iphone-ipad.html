iPhone / iPad Version

This option helps you install Prayer Room like an app on your Home Screen.

Open Prayer Room

If you don’t want to install, you can still use it normally in Safari.

Install on iPhone/iPad (Safari):

  1. Open Prayer Room (button above)
  2. Tap Share
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Name it “Prayer Room” → tap Add

If the Share menu doesn’t show “Add to Home Screen”, scroll down inside the Share sheet.
