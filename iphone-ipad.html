Prayer Room — iPhone / iPad

This page is for iPhone and iPad users.

Open the Prayer Room web app:

Open Prayer Room

Install to Home Screen (recommended):

  1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome).
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow).
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open it from your Home Screen like an app.

If you get sent back to the start page, just open iphone-ipad.html again and use the button above.
